About Us

About TJ Quizbowl

TJHSST Quizbowl is a team which fosters learning of all academic and non-academic interests through competitions. TJ Quizbowl is nationally renowned and competes in tournaments across the nation. We hold practices on Wednesdays 8B and Fridays 8B, where players of all skill levels are welcome. We also have candy at practices, so be sure to show up if you happen to have a sweet tooth!

Practices are Wednesday and Friday B block, and Monday and Friday during lunch. There may be supplemental practices prior to important tournaments. Practices typically consist of reading past tournament questions. We have two rooms for eighth periods - a novice room, which reads easier questions, and a more experienced room. We usually read college level questions at lunch practice.

National tournaments
Competitions

TJQB attends a variety of competitions, both local and national throughout the year, culminating in the VHSL and national championships. Tournaments are a great way to tour the DMV area as well as show academic prowess at all levels.

Extracurricular activities
Practices

TJQB conducts annual summer practices, usually at a member's house, to continue to foster the growth of academic learning over summer break, as well as introduce new freshmen to TJQB. TJQB also hosts weekend online practices in the discord.

Hosted tournaments
TJ NAT, MST, IAT

Each year, TJQB hosts three Quizbowl tournaments of their own at varying levels (novice, middle school, and invitational). These tournaments serve as fundraising events and valuable outreach opportunities.

Club activities
8th Period

TJQB eighth periods are Wednesday and Friday B blocks, and typically serve as perfect time for practices. There are packets of varying difficulties open to everyone. There will additionally be lectures announced ahead of time.
