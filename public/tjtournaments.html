TJ Tournaments

Each year, TJQB hosts three Quizbowl tournaments of their own at varying levels (novice, middle school, and invitational). These tournaments serve as fundraising events and valuable outreach opportunities. 

2022-2023 Announcement

TJQB is tentatively planning on a return to in-person tournament hosting alongside expanded online tournaments. Check back in September for information about our first online tournament!

Volunteering

Members can staff these tournaments for community service hours. The tournaments serve as fundraising events and valuable outreach opportunities. Staffers may choose to read questions, scorekeep, or sell concessions.
